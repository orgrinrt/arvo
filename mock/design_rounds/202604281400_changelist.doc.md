## Drift 1. `arvo/DESIGN.md.tmpl` `Bits<const N: u8>` claim

`mock/crates/arvo/DESIGN.md.tmpl`
- Section: "Meta-bit newtypes" (around lines 163 to 167).
- Replace the paragraph that asserts substrate uses 0..=255 because
  `Bits<const N: u8>` itself remains at u8.
- New paragraph states: 9-bit native range covers 0..=511. Round B raised
  the substrate cap to 255 (the limit of `Bits<const N: u8>` at the time);
  Round 202604281000 Pass F widened that const-generic to `u16`, so the
  reachable cap is now 0..=65535 in principle. Container dispatch covers
  up to 255 today (single-primitive 65..=128 -> `u128`; multi-primitive
  129..=255 -> `MultiContainer`). Cells beyond 255 are BACKLOG-tracked.
  The single bottom-out point for `u16` const-generics is
  `Bits<const N: u16, S, Sign>` itself; meta-layer types (`IBits`,
  `FBits`, `Width`) lift to typed fields above it via `MetaCarrier`.

The change preserves the surrounding paragraph's structure (intro of the
9-bit native range, followed by the bottom-out point, followed by the
meta-layer lift). Only the content of the cap claim and the const-generic
type changes. The `design-doc-source-mismatch` lint will treat the new
phrasing as authoritative once the Pass F-shipped source matches.
