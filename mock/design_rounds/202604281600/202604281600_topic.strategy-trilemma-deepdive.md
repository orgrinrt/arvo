## Why

The trilemma research note has been load-bearing context for several rounds
in the audit chain. Round 202604271346 referenced it. Round 202604281000
Pass G shipped `CrossStrategyOp<S1, S2>` with `#[diagnostic::on_unimplemented]`,
whose entire design rationale rests on the trilemma's "trait-first, alias-first,
never bit-width-generic at the consumer surface" discipline. The substrate's
generic-over-width algorithm crates (arvo-graph, arvo-sparse, arvo-spectral,
arvo-comb) all internalise the layer-3 friction that the trilemma describes.

Today the note lives at `mock/research/`. Research notes are not gated by
the writing-style lint, are not part of the rendered docs surface, and do
not surface in `cargo mock` doc generation. They are scratch-pad investigation
material. The trilemma has earned promotion: it explains a structural
constraint that ships in every UFixed/IFixed/Bits use site, and it sets a
discipline that the workspace `cookbook.md` rule and the Pass G diagnostic
both rely on without saying out loud.

DEEPDIVE templates are the right home for this material. They render into
canonical docs alongside DESIGN.md, are subject to writing-style and
design-doc lints, and are read by consumers trying to understand why the
substrate makes the design choices it does. Existing precedent:
`DEEPDIVE_newtype-deref.md.tmpl` covers a similar scope (a structural
choice with workspace-wide consequences and a discipline that consumers need
to follow).

  updates to reflect the current substrate state.
- Not a new substrate principle. The trilemma is an existing constraint

## Decisions

- **Decision 1**: keep the research file at `mock/research/strategy-bound-trilemma.md`
  with a "promoted" pointer at the top. Do not delete. Audit trail is
  load-bearing across the workspace per the preserve-audit-trail principle.

- **Decision 2**: the DEEPDIVE template uses the title pattern `# arvo. Deep
  Dive: The Strategy-Bound Trilemma` to match the existing
  `DEEPDIVE_newtype-deref.md.tmpl` shape. Title in source is `# arvo.
  Deep Dive: Deref on Primitive Newtypes`; the trilemma's pattern follows
  the same `arvo. Deep Dive: <topic title-cased>` form.

- **Decision 3**: DEEPDIVE source reflects post-Pass-F state. The note's
  layer-3 example uses `<const I: IBits, const F: FBits, S: Strategy>`
  generics and the where-clause in `ufixed_bits(I, F)` form that is current
  on `dev`. No need to retain pre-Pass-F u8 const-generic phrasing in the
  shipping doc.
