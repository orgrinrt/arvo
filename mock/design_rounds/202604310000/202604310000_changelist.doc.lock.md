## `mock/crates/arvo-bits/DESIGN.md.tmpl`

### "Opaque-bit container: Bits" — add `From` conversion paragraph

After the `Bits::from_raw` / `Bits::new` description, add:

> `From` impls land two conversion paths for ergonomic non-const call
> sites: `impl<N, S> From<<S as UContainerFor<N>>::T> for Bits<N, S>`
> (the exact-container generic) and `impl From<u64> for Bits<N, Hot>`
> (per-N via macro; mask-then-narrow). Consumers in non-const
> positions write `let b: Bits<N, Hot> = value.into()` or
> `Bits::from(value)`. Const paths (`str_const!()` macro bodies,
> bitfield setters) keep the explicit `Bits::<N>::new(raw)` form —
> `From::from` isn't `const fn` on current nightly.

### "Bitfield — packed layouts with named sub-ranges" — add mask-constant note

Append to the inherent-methods description:

> The macro also generates a pre-shifted `<FIELD_UPPER>_MASK: Bits<N, Hot>`
> associated constant per sub-range. For boundary bit-arithmetic
> (consumer code that holds raw values before wrapping them) the mask
> saves redeclaring the layout's internal structure. For example,
> `StrLayout` generates `ORIGIN_MASK`, `RESERVED_MASK`, `ID_MASK` at
> the full 32-bit width.
>
> `SHIFT` and `WIDTH` constants are not generated — consumers that
> need them can compute from the field declaration inline; avoiding
> them prevents speculative-pub-const bloat.
